# 🎭 Digital Storytelling Projects
## 📌 Overview
This repository contains a collection of digital storytelling projects developed as part of my Multimedia studies at UOC. The work explores interactive narratives, user engagement and experience design through different creative formats.

---

## 📚 Projects Included

### 🧩 Interactive Escape Room (Edgar Allan Poe)
A narrative-driven escape room inspired by the works of Edgar Allan Poe, designed to create an immersive and interactive user experience.

### 📱 Social Media Storytelling (Anne Frank)
A digital storytelling experience created around the commemoration of Anne Frank, designed for engagement through social media platforms.

### 🌱 Transmedia Project ("Drets? De Segona")
A transmedia campaign combining a physical exhibition and digital experience that encourages reflection on the ethics of animal food production and promotes critical thinking about dietary choices.
